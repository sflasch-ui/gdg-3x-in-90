Pillar 3

Governance by Design

Embedding globally standardized risk standards and AI-driven delivery intelligence into field workflows

Objective
Make governance invisible to consultants by embedding globally standardized risk definitions and proactive AI-driven risk intelligence directly into delivery workflows — so compliance and risk awareness are a natural outcome of how consultants work, not an external layer they manage separately.
Baseline
No globally standardized definitions for project or portfolio risk exist in documented form. Delivery leadership has no proactive risk signal mechanism. Consultants navigate governance requirements manually with no embedded guidance. Business requirements for a Proactive Risk Agent have not been authored.
Target
Global standard project and portfolio risk definitions consolidated, documented, and published across all GEOs. Delivery metrics from existing Metric Dictionary incorporated into the risk library as a unified framework. Foundation established for Proactive Risk Agent development (longer-term initiative, beyond 90-day window).
Lever
Claude and Slackbot used to consolidate existing risk documentation into a unified framework. Cross-GEO alignment process to validate shared risk language with Geo Leads. Delivery metrics folded into risk library rather than maintained as a standalone obligation. Innovation partnership engaged for longer-term Risk Agent roadmap.
90-Day Outcome
Global risk definitions (project and portfolio) consolidated using AI, documented, validated with Geo Leads, and published. Delivery metrics incorporated into the risk library as foundational inputs. Proactive Risk Agent scoped in longer-term planning with Innovation — not a 90-day deliverable, but partnership and roadmap position confirmed.
Efficiency Gains Extended To
All GEOs (risk standardization reduces ambiguity and escalation overhead). Practices, Offers (methodology alignment). Innovation (clear BRs accelerate build and reduce rework).
Confidence
Medium-High (risk definitions — AI-assisted consolidation already underway)  ·  Medium (Risk Agent — longer-term, dependent on Innovation roadmap)
Pillar 4

Internal Productivity

Reclaiming the GDG team's strategic capacity by replacing manual overhead with AI-enabled operations

Objective
Replace the reactive, manual overhead consuming the GDG team's strategic capacity — unstructured intake, slow BR writing, constant stakeholder re-alignment, undeflected BAU, and untapped automation reuse — with AI-assisted workflows that free approximately 1 FTE-equivalent of weekly capacity for roadmap execution.
Baseline
No structured intake process (requests triaged reactively, no BVR scoring). BR writing takes up to 5 days (~20% of one team member's weekly capacity per BR). No published Living Roadmap, generating ~8 hpw/week in recurring stakeholder alignment. ~12 hpw/week consumed by BAU support. No team prompt library or shared AI context file. CS automation inventory never assessed for delivery reuse. Estimated recoverable overhead: 20+ hpw/week.
Target
Intake process live with AI-assisted BVR scoring. BR cycle reduced from 5 days to 1-2 days (60-80% reduction). Living Roadmap published and self-serve, reclaiming ~8 hpw/week. BAU deflected via AI tooling, reclaiming ~10 hpw/week. Prompt library and "About Us" context file live. CS automation AI audit completed. Total target: ~1 FTE-equivalent of capacity reclaimed per week.
Lever
Structured intake workflow with AI-assisted BVR scoring. Claude + Cursor BR drafting templates (replacing 5-day manual cycle). Living Roadmap published on cadence. AI-enabled BAU deflection tools. Team "About Us" context file and shared prompt library for consistent AI output across all 5 team members. AI-powered audit of CS automation inventory to identify automations portable to delivery processes.
90-Day Outcome
Intake process designed, documented, and live. BR drafting workflow AI-assisted and averaging 1-2 day cycle time. Living Roadmap published and updating on cadence, eliminating ad hoc alignment conversations. BAU deflection toolkit deployed with hpw tracking in place. Team prompt library and "About Us" context file in active use by all 5 team members. AI audit of CS automation inventory completed — portable candidates identified and recommendation handed to transformation team.
Efficiency Gains Extended To
GDG Team (primary). Innovation (faster BRs reduces build rework). Customer Success (automation reuse). Field (governance frameworks delivered faster as a downstream effect).
Confidence
High (intake, BR workflow, Living Roadmap, prompt library — all within team control)  ·  Medium (BAU deflection, CS audit)
